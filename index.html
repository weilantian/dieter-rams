(Industrial Designer)

Dieter Rams

WORKS

A set of products Dieter Rams made during the years of working for german company Braun.

SK 5 phonosuper

Successor to the groundbreaking SK 4 and SK 4 1/a, LW reception is added to the FM and MW range of the earlier models. A screen printed tuning dial on metal plate replaces the printed paper.

TG 60

Reel-to-reel player/ recorder with white housing, compatible with audio 1/2/350 and TS 45.

ET 23

Beautiful brown / black / yellow keyed arithmetic calculator with 8 digits precision and algebraic logic. The pre-lcd vacuum fluorescent display shows green numerals.

T3

Early pocket radio of '58, recently much compared with the first generation Apple iPod.

Phase 1

Very scarce version of the phase 1 clock with acrylic housing and battery operation.
